Plot Hooks

From Our observations, although please remember we don’t see everything, plot hooks are not so much a tool Game Masters beat players into line with, but an unspoken agreement for everyone involved in the campaign to move forward so enjoyment can be had by all. With that in mind if the whole party is onboard you can be blatant and possibly even jokey with your Plot hooks. As a future development this area could be expanded and user discussions involved to the benefit of us all. For now lets look at 2 potential fun plot hooks to engage both players and Game Masters.

help wanted poster

A poster that when touched triggers a message spell that describes the quests goals and rewards.

mysterious Stranger

A mysterious stranger that attracts the attention of the party to describe the quest.
