Collect Bat Guano

Location

In the hills above the orchard by the river is a cave system where bats are prolific. To get there the party needs to follow the river past the orchard where they must find the track to cave system.

Layout

Basiclly there is only 6 encounter areas these are as follows.

  1. Entrance
  2. First room with wide exit to the left and narrow exit to the right on top of the entrance used.
  3. Second room from the wide exit with 2 wide exits left and right on top of the entrance.
  4. Third room from the left exit temimal
  5. Forth room from the wide right exit terminal
  6. Fifth room the guano room

Adversaries

  1. Entrance

    2 no. Goblin guards

  2. First room

    4 no. Goblin raiders

  3. Second room

    4 no. Goblin acolytes

  4. Third room

    2 no. goblin house guards 1 no. hobgoblin boss

  5. Forth room

    Booty storage Gold 2 no. weapons Potions

  6. Fifth room

    4 no. swarms of bats woken if party is noisy
